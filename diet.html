🥜 Healthy Fats

Avocados, nuts, and seeds help absorb nutrients and are excellent with heart medications.

🥛 Dairy Alternatives

For those on iron or thyroid meds, consider calcium-alternative milks like almond or soy.

🥦 Vegetables

Boost immunity and liver function – excellent for most medications.

🍚 Whole Grains

Supports blood sugar regulation – especially with diabetic meds.
