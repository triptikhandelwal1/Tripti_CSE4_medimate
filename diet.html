Smart Eating for a Healthier You

  • Eat more fruits 🍎, vegetables 🥦, and whole grains 🌾.
  • Limit processed foods and sugary drinks 🥤.
  • Include protein from plant or lean animal sources 🐟🥚.
  • Don’t skip meals! Try to eat at regular times ⏰.
  • Read labels carefully – watch for added sugar and salt 🧂.
  • Choose healthy fats like nuts 🥜, seeds, and olive oil 🫒.
  • Make water your best friend – hydrate regularly 💧.
  • Listen to your body – eat when you’re hungry and stop when full 🧠.
  • Enjoy your meals and eat mindfully 🍽️.
  • Balance is key! No food is off-limits in moderation 🎯.
