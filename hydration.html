Why Water is Important 💦

Water is essential to maintain your body's functions. Staying hydrated helps regulate temperature, keeps your joints lubricated, and aids in digestion and nutrient absorption.

Drinking enough water daily can also improve your energy levels, focus, and overall health. Adults should aim for 8–10 glasses a day!

🕒 Tip: Set reminders every 2 hours to drink a glass of water!
